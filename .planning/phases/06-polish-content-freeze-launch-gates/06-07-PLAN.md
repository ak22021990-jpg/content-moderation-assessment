<objective>
Redesign the GuidelinesScreen so the taxonomy is less text-heavy and more interactive. L1 category cards collapse by default. Clicking a card expands it and reveals its full definition and L2 sub-categories in a dedicated right-side detail panel, using the empty space beside the cards. Each L1 card gets a custom SVG icon.

Purpose: Improve information density and readability; candidates can scan categories quickly and drill into details without overwhelming walls of text.

Output: Refactored GuidelinesScreen with accordion + side panel + icons.
</objective>
